Data Cleaning in SQL

This step in data preparation involves handling missing values, removing duplicate entries, correcting typos, standardizing formats, and addressing other issues that could compromise the quality and reliability of the data. The goal of data cleaning is to ensure that the dataset is accurate, complete, and ready for analysis, thus enhancing the overall integrity of the information and supporting more reliable insights and decision-making.
